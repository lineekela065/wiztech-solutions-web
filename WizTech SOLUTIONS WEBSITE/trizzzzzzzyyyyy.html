My Story

Born on July 7, 2004, in Engela, Namibia, I've always been fascinated by technology and design. From fixing phones in my community to developing complex software systems, my journey has been driven by curiosity and a passion for creating digital solutions.

As an Oshikwanyama-speaking member of the Wambo tribe, I bring a unique cultural perspective to my work. Currently in my third year studying Computer Science (Software Development) at NUST, I'm honing my skills to become a well-rounded tech professional.

Beyond coding, I'm an avid learner of business management principles and enjoy applying design thinking methodologies to solve real-world problems.
